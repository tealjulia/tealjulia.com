About

I'm Teal Larson, a Full Stack Web Developer based near Tacoma, WA.

I wake up excited to tackle my latest projects. Currently, I'm doing both freelance and volunteer work for everyone from a local birth center to a history museum. I believe in the power of programming to change the world for the better by helping people achieve more. I enjoy building intuitive web apps that solve real-world needs. My BA is in Language, Literacy, and Cultural Studies, and my passion for human interaction and learning inform everything I build from UIs to APIs.

If I'm not coding, I am probably on a hike with my sons, whipping up something in the kitchen, or nose-deep in a book.
